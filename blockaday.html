Project Overview

Blockaday is a community-based Web3 EduTech company. We find innovative ways to provide curated education for individuals and organizations on the potential of blockchain technology. We present this content simply, inclusively and honestly, so a diverse audience can understand, experience and learn by doing.

Problem

We believe that blockchain technology has the potential to empower billions of lives. However, while this technology has gone mainstream, it is still deeply misunderstood.

The potential reach of organizations in Web3 is limited by their customers’ ability to understand the technology and the organization’s capacity to create educational content about their product and Web3 in general.

Solution

This educational content creation can be outsourced to Blockaday, so builders can focus on building and executives can focus on business strategy.

We create Web3 education tailored specifically to your company, informing your potential customers about your product and how to use it. We will meet with you to understand your content needs and the needs of your users, then design a custom content delivery plan for you. We take care of the entire content creation process.

Here are some of the possible delivery methods:

  • Survey
  • Survey Analysis
  • Information Architecture
  • User Task Flow
  • Prototyping
  • Hi-fidelity Wireframes
